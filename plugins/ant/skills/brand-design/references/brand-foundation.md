# Brand Foundation

Source: public `(ant)` Visualbook export generated on 2026-06-03. Use `source-ant-brand.md` for exact manual text and `../assets/source/manifest.json` for machine-readable asset metadata.

## Identity Summary

`(ant)` is a full-service digital agency identity built around sharp contrast, practical craft, and visible authored work. The recognizable signals are:

- the `(ant)` wordmark and bracket language;
- black/white editorial structure;
- mint green as the signature accent;
- extended headline typography;
- short expressive labels such as `(WOW)`, `(WHY)`, `(HOT)`, `(FUN)`;
- the `(ant) crafted` endorsement system.

## Secondary Colors

Secondary colors are for UI/web support, not brand dominance:

- `#ffe4c1` light brown;
- `#dad4ff` light purple;
- `#f4ca99` dark brown;
- `#fffd92` yellow;
- `#ffe2eb` pink;
- `#d7edff` light blue.

Use them sparingly for categorization, diagrams, small cards, or state differentiation. Do not make a page primarily pastel unless the brief specifically asks for a campaign treatment and the `(ant)` signals remain strong.

## Typography

Primary headline font:

- Aktiv Grotesk EX;
- weights 400, 500, 600, 700;
- use for headlines, hero statements, section titles, large numeric/label statements, and key brand marks.

Body/UI font:

- Inter;
- weights 300, 400, 500, 600, 700;
- use for body copy, controls, labels, UI tables, supporting text, documents, and long content.

Fallback:

- Windows: Arial;
- macOS: Helvetica / San Francisco.

If Aktiv Grotesk EX is not installed or licensed in the target project, use the existing project heading font or a neutral extended/geometric substitute only after stating the limitation. Do not imply that a substitute is the official brand font.

## Logo Rules

Bundled logo files:

- `../assets/logos/logo-light-primarni-logo-ant.svg` for light backgrounds;
- `../assets/logos/logo-dark-primarni-logo-ant.svg` for dark backgrounds;
- `../assets/logos/logo-light-logo-s-claimem.svg` for light backgrounds with claim;
- `../assets/logos/logo-dark-logo-s-claimem.svg` for dark backgrounds with claim.

Minimum height:

- primary logo: 14 px on screens, 5 mm in print;
- logo with claim: 16 px on screens, 20 mm in print.

Allowed background colors mentioned for primary logo usage include `#5bffc4`, `#000000`, `#ffffff`, `#e7e8e9`, `#aeb1b7`. The manual flags some color-background combinations as forbidden; consult `source-ant-brand.md` before using the logo on nonstandard colored backgrounds.

Do not distort, recolor, outline, rotate, crop, or recreate the logo.

## Endorsement

`(ant) crafted` is the authorship signature:

- use in website footers and final frames when the output was produced by `(ant)`;
- use `(ant) crafted for ...` for premium/top project endorsement;
- dynamic variants can include creator, version, city, or output type, but treat those as internal/contextual concepts unless the user asks.
